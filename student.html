Assistant Director: Work Program

The school operates a number of auxiliary and vocational services where students may work also at school to earn some money in order to defray some of their school expenses. These opportunities, to engage in productive and useful labor, help develop character traits of industry, dependability, and initiative. In the process, students may also acquire valuable vocational skills.
